Band Overview

Incubus is an american rock band - THE American rock band - from Calabasas, California. The band was born in a gargae in 1991 by vocalist Brandon Boyd, lead guitarist Mike Einziger, and drummer Jose Pasillas while enrolled in Calabasas High School. They existed as a band for some time before giving themselves a name out merely out of neccesity, testament to the laid-back vibe their speakers gift the world with every time we press play. Widely accepted rumor says Incubus came up with their name last minute, mere days before they took the stage for a gig at a local bar in Calabasas.

The band's early shows were frequently at nightclubs on Los Angeles' Sunset Strip, including well-known venues such as the Whisky a Go Go, The Roxy and the Troubadour. Several years after inception, the band expanded to include bassist Alex "Dirk Lance" Katunic and Gavin "DJ Lyfe" Koppell; both of whom were eventually replaced by bassist Ben Kenney and DJ Kilmore respectively.

Incubus has reached commercial success, going platinum many times over, as well as releasing several successful singles. Singles like Drive, Pardon Me, and Stellar have rocketed Incubus to the tips of most Rock-savy individuals,

Athough their last album release - If Not Now, When? - hit the shelves in 2011, frontman Brandon Boyd recently announced via twitter that the band would go on tour and release a full album, chockfull of the songs we've all grown to love.
